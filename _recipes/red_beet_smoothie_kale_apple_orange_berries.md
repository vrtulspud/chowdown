ingredients:
- 1 Red Beet
- 1 small Apple(s)
- 1/2 Orange, Navel (peeled)
- 1 handful Berries (frozen)
- 1 handful Kale
- 1/4 cup Pineapple (frozen)
- 1/2 cup Ice
- 1/2 cup Water, Iced







directions:
- Put all ingredients into blender. 
- Start on slow and move up to high, using the muddler the machine comes with.
